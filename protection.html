How To Stay Safe

There are many simple ways to stay safe online. Good online habits can help protect your accounts, personal information, and devices.

Use strong passwords

Choose passwords that are long and hard to guess. Try to include numbers and symbols.

Do not share personal information

Be careful with your phone number, password, address, and other private details.

Check links before clicking

Some links can take you to fake or dangerous websites. Always check before you click.

Use two-factor authentication

This adds extra security to your account and makes it harder for someone to get in.

Keep apps and devices updated

Updates can fix security problems and help keep your device safe.
