Online Threats

Online threats are dangers on the internet that can harm your device or steal your information. Some common threats include malware, phishing, and unsafe downloads. Some definitions are below:

Malware

Malware is harmful software that can damage your computer.

Phishing

Phishing tricks users into giving personal information.

Unsafe Downloads

Downloading files from unknown websites can be dangerous.
